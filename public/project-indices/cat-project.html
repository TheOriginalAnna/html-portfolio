Cats of the World

Brown Cats

Brown cats have soft brown fur which can be short, medium, or long. They are good boys and girls who love cuddles and kisses.


Black Cats

Although often misunderstood, black cats are beautiful sweet animals with lots of love to give. They enjoy hunting, playing, and sleeping.


Orange Cats

Orange cats are lovely kitty cat creatures. They are playful and loving, enjoying long naps in the sun and all the catnip.
